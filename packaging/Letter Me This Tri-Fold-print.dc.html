What You Need
1 Alphabet Die
1 Number Die
Paper and pencils (one per player)
A group of friends ready to play
Timer or phone
Setup
1
Sit in a circle.
2
Everyone grabs paper and a pencil.
3
Pick someone to be The Subject — the person everyone will describe this round.
4
Place both dice in the center.
How to Play
1
Roll the Dice
Player to the left of The Subject rolls both dice. Letter Die = the starting letter for all your words. Number Die = how many words to write.
Example: Roll R and 3 → write three words starting with R that describe The Subject.
2
Write Fast
Set a 90-second timer. Everyone except The Subject writes words privately. When time's up, stop writing. Only play the number of words on the die for that round. Cross out any extra words before the reveal.
3
Reveal & Score
Go around the circle reading words aloud. The Subject approves real words that describe them — 1 point each. If two players wrote the same word, nobody scores it.
4
Rotate
The player to the left becomes the next Subject. Play until everyone has been The Subject at least once. Highest score wins — or just play for laughs.
Scoring & Winning
Scoring
1 point per unique, approved word
Use a scorekeeper or tally your own
Winning
Most points at the end wins
Or just play for laughs
What's an Approved Word?
✓A real word (in the dictionary)
✓Truly describes The Subject — even if unflattering
✓Minor misspellings still count — it's a party game, not a spelling bee
✓Different words score separately — “loving” and “lovable” both count
When in doubt, majority rules.
Quick Game Tip
Play until everyone's had a turn — or go as many rounds as you want!
